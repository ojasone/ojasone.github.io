Everything You Need to Collect Data

From simple lists to complex contact management, Sangraha has you covered.

Offline First

Access and manage all your sheet data even without an internet connection. Your data is stored securely on your device.

Dual Mode Import

Use your Google Account or an API key for full-featured access. No key? No problem. The app smartly switches to CSV mode for public links.

Contact Management

Save contacts directly from your sheets to your phone in bulk. Configure naming rules, prefixes, and starting indexes with ease.

Customizable Views

You decide what to see. Configure which columns are displayed and what type of data they represent (headings, phone numbers, links, etc.).

Feature-Driven Cards

Enable features like checklists, one-tap copying, and sharing on a per-sheet basis to make your data interactive and actionable.

Organize with Groups

Tidy up your main screen by organizing sheets into collapsible groups. Perfect for managing multiple projects or categories.
