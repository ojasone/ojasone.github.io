Privacy Policy for Sangraha

Effective Date: October 7, 2025

1. Information We Collect and Use

Sangraha is designed to be an offline-first data utility. All data you import and settings you configure are stored exclusively on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to our servers. Information is handled in the following ways:

a) Information You Provide

  • Google Sheet URLs: You provide URLs to Google Sheets that you wish to import and view. These are stored locally.
  • Configuration Data: Settings like API keys and display preferences are stored locally. Your API key is only used to communicate with Google's APIs as you direct.

b) Information from Google Services

  • Google Sign-In: Your email address is received for display purposes only if you choose to sign in.
  • Google Sheets Data: Data from your sheets is downloaded and stored in a local database on your device for offline access.

c) Information from Your Device (with Your Permission)

To save data as new contacts on your device, the app will explicitly ask for your permission to read and write contacts.

Why we need it:

This is required solely to create or delete contacts previously created by the app, as you direct.

What we DO NOT do:

The app **does not** read, collect, or upload your existing contact list. It only interacts with the contacts you explicitly ask it to manage.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your Google Sheet data within the app.
  • To enable offline access to your imported data.
  • To save your settings and preferences on your device.
  • To create or delete contacts when you use the contact management feature.
  • To authenticate with Google services for private sheets.

3. Data Storage & Security

All your data is stored in a private, local database on your device, which is deleted if you uninstall Sangraha. While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We **do not** sell, trade, or share any of your personal information or sheet data. All data remains on your device. The app communicates directly with Google's APIs, and your interaction with those services is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

5. App Permissions

INTERNET

Required to connect to Google Sheets to download your data.

READ & WRITE CONTACTS

Required only for the optional feature that allows you to save and delete contacts. The app will explicitly ask for these permissions.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
